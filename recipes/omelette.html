Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2g butter
  • Vegetables
  • salt & pepper
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Heat the butter in a non stick frying pan. Add the chopped onions, vegetable of your choices. Give it a mix. Season to taste.
  2. Pour in the egg mix in the pan and set it on medium heat till it sets, Use a large spatula to swirl it in the pan, set the temperature to medium heat and swirl for 30 seconds maximum
  3. There you will have a delicious omelette ready to be served!
