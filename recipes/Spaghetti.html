Spaghetti is beloved by everyone because of its sweetness and how delicious it is, is is easy to prepare and sastify hungerness

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground meat of your choice
  • tomatoes and tomato paste
  • onion, garlic, and spices

Steps

  1. Brown your meat in a bit of oil, and then stir in onions until they soften. Then add lots of flavor with garlic, tomato paste, oregano, and red pepper flakes. To finish the sauce, add a bit of water (or broth) and crushed tomatoes. Our easy sauce simmers for about 25 minutes, so about 15 minutes before it is ready, you can cook your spaghetti.
  2. Cook spaghetti in salted(abt 1 teaspoon of salt) boiled water, cook the spaghetti for 8 minutes(check the spaghetti details to see if it needs longer)
  3. Stir the pasta sauce and spaghetti together and enjoy!
