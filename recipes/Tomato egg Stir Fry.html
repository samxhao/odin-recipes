Tomato egg stir-fry is a very common delicious Chinese dish that is easy to cook with everyday ingredient

Ingredients

  • 4 small to medium tomatoes
  • 1 scallion
  • 4 eggs
  • 3/4 tsp salted
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper
  • 1 tsp shaoxing wine
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Start by cutting tomatoes into small wedges and finely chop the scallion
  2. Crack 4 eggs into a bowl and season with salt, white pepper, sesame oil, and Shaoxing wine. Neat eggs for a minute.
  3. Preheat the wok over medium heat until it just starts to smoke. Then add 2 tablespoons of oil and immediately add the eggs. Scramble the eggs and remove from the wok immediately. Set aside
  4. Add 1 more tablespoon oil to the wok, turn up the heat to high, and add the tomatoes and scallions. Stir-fry for 1 minute, and then add sugar, salt and water. Add the cooked eggs.
  5. Mix everything together, cover the wok, and cook for 1-2 minutes, until the tomatoes are completely softened.
  6. Unciver, and continue to stir-fry over high heat until the sauce thickens to your liking. Serve!
